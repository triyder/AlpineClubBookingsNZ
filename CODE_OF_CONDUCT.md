# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple contributor standard:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but make technical concerns explicit.
- Keep discussion focused on the code, documentation, security, and operational
  behaviour of the project.
- Do not harass, threaten, demean, or publish private information about others.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that do not
meet this standard. Serious or repeated violations may result in blocked
participation.
